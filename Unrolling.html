Step 1: Choose one algorithm (Binary Search, Merge Sort, Fibbonacci).

For Binary Search:
  • Drag the circle in the slider to select the number we want to find from our sorted array.
  • The function should give us back the index of the element we chose from the slider.
  • Click on the expand buttons to see the full unrolled tree that makes up BinaryS(x)

For Fibonacci:
  • Drag the circle in the slider to increase the amount of input we will put into the function.
  • Click on the expand buttons to see the full unrolled tree that makes up Fib(x)
  • The output of Fib(x) is the result of the recursive Fibbonacci sequence.
